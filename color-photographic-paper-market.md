<p><p>The color photographic paper market primarily consists of two types: silver halide photographic paper and inkjet photographic paper. Silver halide paper relies on chemical processing to produce high-quality images and is favored for traditional photography due to its exceptional color reproduction and archival qualities. In contrast, inkjet photographic paper is designed for use with inkjet printers, providing versatility and convenience for digital printing. Both types cater to different consumer needs, from professional photographers to hobbyists seeking vibrant prints.</p></p>
