<p><p>The zinc target market is primarily divided into two types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, stationary surfaces used in applications like sputtering for thin film deposition, offering uniformity in coating. Conversely, rotating targets have a cylindrical design, enabling continuous exposure of fresh material during operation, which enhances efficiency and consistency in various industrial applications. Both target types are essential in producing zinc coatings that improve product durability and performance in diverse sectors such as electronics and automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
