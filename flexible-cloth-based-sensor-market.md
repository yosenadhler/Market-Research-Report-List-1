<p><p>The flexible cloth-based sensor market encompasses various types, primarily pressure sensors and tension sensors. Pressure sensors detect changes in force applied to the fabric, making them ideal for applications in healthcare and wearable technology, where monitoring vital signs is crucial. Tension sensors, on the other hand, measure the degree of stretch or strain in the material, useful for fitness tracking and structural health monitoring. Both sensor types leverage the unique properties of flexible materials to provide accurate, real-time data in diverse applications.</p></p>
