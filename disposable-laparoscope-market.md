<p><p>The disposable laparoscope market includes various types of instruments designed for minimally invasive surgical procedures. Bipolar instruments are used for cutting and coagulating tissue with controlled energy, while monopolar instruments offer a single-active electrode for electrosurgery. Trocars serve as entry points for other instruments, facilitating access to the surgical site. Graspers enable the manipulation and holding of tissues, whereas dissectors are employed to separate and dissect tissues efficiently. Together, these tools enhance surgical precision and reduce infection risk.</p></p>
