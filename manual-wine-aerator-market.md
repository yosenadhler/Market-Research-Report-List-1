<p><p>The manual wine aerator market consists of two primary types: in-bottle wine aerators and handheld wine aerators. Handheld wine aerators, on the other hand, are used during pouring to aerate the wine immediately, creating a better drinking experience by increasing oxygen exposure in real-time. Both types cater to wine enthusiasts seeking improved taste.</p></p>
