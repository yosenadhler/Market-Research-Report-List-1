<p><p>The energy storage market for solid-state batteries is primarily categorized into all-solid-state batteries (ASSBs) and semi-solid-state batteries. ASSBs utilize solid electrolytes throughout, offering enhanced safety and energy density. They are ideal for applications requiring high performance and longevity. Conversely, semi-solid-state batteries combine liquid and solid components, aiming to balance energy density and manufacturability while mitigating some safety concerns associated with fully liquid systems. Both types target electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and renewable energy storage solutions.</p></p>
