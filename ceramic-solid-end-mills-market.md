<p><p>The Ceramic Solid End Mills market can be categorized into three main types based on metal alloys. Iron-based ceramic end mills are predominantly used for general machining applications, offering good wear resistance and strength. Nickel-based variants are ideal for high-temperature applications, providing excellent stability and resistance to thermal deformation. Cobalt-based end mills excel in toughness and are particularly effective in machining tough materials, making them suitable for specialized industrial applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on performance requirements.</p></p>
