<p><p>Balsa core market is categorized into two main types: monolayer and multilayer. The monolayer market features a single layer of balsa wood, offering lightweight and cost-effective solutions, ideal for applications requiring basic strength and insulation. In contrast, the multilayer market consists of multiple balsa layers laminated together, providing enhanced structural integrity and performance. This layered approach is preferred in high-performance applications, such as aerospace and marine industries, where strength-to-weight ratio and durability are critical.</p></p>
