Microdroplet separation encases single cells in droplets for analysis, facilitating high-throughput screening. Microporous separation involves membranes with tiny pores that selectively capture cells based on size or properties. Combined labeling integrates multiple techniques for enhanced identification and tracking of cells. Other market types may include emerging methods and technologies that further improve single-cell analysis efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
