<p><p>The Electrical Submersible Pump (ESP) power cable market primarily features two types of insulation materials: Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) and Polypropylene. EPDM is known for its excellent resistance to heat, ozone, and water, making it ideal for harsh downhole environments. In contrast, Polypropylene offers lightweight properties and good dielectric strength, often preferred for more flexible applications. Both serve essential roles in ensuring reliable power transmission to ESPs, essential for efficient oil and gas extraction.</p></p>
