<p><strong>The TFT LCD Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twisted Nematic (TN)</li><li>In-Plane Switching (IPS)</li><li>Advanced Fringe Field Switching (AFFS)</li><li>Multi-Domain Vertical Alignment (MVA)</li><li>Patterned Vertical Alignment (PVA)</li><li>Advanced Super View (ASV)</li><li>Plane Line Switching (PLS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TFT LCD display market encompasses various technologies, each offering distinct advantages. Twisted Nematic (TN) displays are known for fast response times and affordability but have limited viewing angles. In-Plane Switching (IPS) provides superior color accuracy and wider viewing angles. Advanced Fringe Field Switching (AFFS) enhances brightness and color performance. Multi-Domain Vertical Alignment (MVA) and Patterned Vertical Alignment (PVA) offer improved contrast ratios and viewing angles. Advanced Super View (ASV) further refines these traits, while Plane Line Switching (PLS) balances performance and cost, alongside other emerging technologies.</p></p>
