<p><ul><li>Particle</li><li>Activated Carbon</li><li>Electrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cabin filters market comprises three main types: particle filters, activated carbon filters, and electrostatic filters. Particle filters primarily trap dust, pollen, and other airborne particles to enhance air quality inside the vehicle. Activated carbon filters not only filter particulates but also absorb odors and harmful gases, providing a more pleasant cabin environment. Electrostatic filters utilize an electric charge to attract and capture particles, offering efficient filtration with lower maintenance needs. Each type serves to improve passenger comfort and health.</p></p>
