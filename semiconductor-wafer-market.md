<p><p>The semiconductor wafer market is divided into two key segments: Front-End-of-Line (FEOL) and Back-End-of-Line (BEOL). FEOL involves initial manufacturing stages, including wafer fabrication, where transistors and other devices are created on the silicon wafer. BEOL, on the other hand, encompasses later stages such as packaging and interconnects, where the chips are assembled and connected to create functional circuits. Together, these segments are crucial for producing efficient and high-performance semiconductor devices essential in various electronic applications.</p></p>
