<p><p>Single scissor lift tables are categorized into three main types based on their power source: electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic. Electric lift tables utilize an electric motor for operation, providing precise control and ease of use in various settings. Hydraulic lift tables employ hydraulic fluid to lift and lower loads, offering high lifting capacity and stability. Pneumatic lift tables rely on compressed air for operation, allowing for quick adjustments and lightweight movement, making them ideal for environments where speed and efficiency are crucial.</p></p>
