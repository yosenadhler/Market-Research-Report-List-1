<p><p>The oxygenator market is primarily divided into two types: General Oxygenators and ECMO Oxygenators. General oxygenators are used in various medical settings for oxygenation during surgeries or respiratory therapies, catering to a broad patient demographic. In contrast, ECMO (Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation) oxygenators are specialized devices used in critical care, providing prolonged life support for patients with severe heart or lung conditions. Both types are essential in enhancing patient care, though they serve distinct clinical needs and applications.</p></p>
