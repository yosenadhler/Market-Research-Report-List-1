<p><p>The ureteral stents market comprises several types, including double pigtail stents, closed-end stents, and multiloop stents. Double pigtail stents feature curled ends that anchor them in the bladder and kidney, facilitating urine flow. Closed-end stents have a sealed tip to prevent backflow and are used for specific obstructions. Multiloop stents incorporate multiple curves, enhancing stability and reducing migration risk. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, ensuring effective management of ureteral obstructions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
