<p><p>The flexible display market encompasses various technologies, including Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), and Electronic Paper Display (EPD). LCDs offer thin, lightweight screens with vibrant colors but generally require backlighting. OLEDs enable deeper blacks and higher contrast, allowing for curved designs. EPDs provide ultra-low power consumption and excellent readability in sunlight, making them ideal for e-readers. Each type addresses unique consumer needs, from visual quality to energy efficiency, fueling diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
