<p><p>The spent fuel and nuclear waste management market encompasses three main types of waste: low-level waste (LLW), intermediate-level waste (ILW), and high-level waste (HLW). LLW contains items like clothing and tools and requires minimal handling and disposal. ILW, including reactor components and chemical waste, needs shielding during storage. HLW consists of used fuel, which generates significant heat and radioactivity, requiring deep geological repositories for safe long-term management. Each waste type demands specific containment, treatment, and disposal strategies.</p></p>
