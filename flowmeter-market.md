<p><p>The flowmeter market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Thermal Type**: This type measures fluid flow by detecting temperature changes, suitable for gases and liquids.</p><p>2. **Mechanical Type**: Utilizing moving parts, such as gears or paddles, it measures flow based on mechanical movement, ideal for various applications.</p><p>3. **Ultrasonic Type**: This employs sound waves to gauge flow velocity, offering non-invasive measurement for liquids.</p><p>4. **Magnetic Type**: Utilizing electromagnetic principles, it measures conductive fluids with high accuracy.</p><p>5. **Pressure Type**: This method assesses flow based on differential pressure, commonly used in industrial settings.</p></p>
