<p><p>The passive sonar system market involves various types, including the multi-beam sonar system and side scan sonar system. Multi-beam sonar systems use multiple beams to map extensive underwater areas, providing detailed, high-resolution images of the seafloor and its features. In contrast, side scan sonar systems operate by emitting sound waves to create images of underwater objects and structures along the seafloor, aiding in navigation and underwater surveillance. Both types play crucial roles in marine research, defense, and exploration.</p></p>
