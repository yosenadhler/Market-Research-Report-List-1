<p><ul><li>Radiofrequency Ablation</li><li>Microwave Ablation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thyroid ablation device market primarily includes two types: radiofrequency ablation (RFA) and microwave ablation (MWA). RFA utilizes high-frequency electromagnetic waves to generate heat, effectively destroying thyroid tissue while minimizing damage to surrounding areas. MWA employs microwave energy to achieve similar results, offering faster treatment times and deeper tissue penetration. Both techniques are minimally invasive, providing alternatives for patients with thyroid nodules or cancers, aimed at reducing the need for thyroid surgery and enhancing patient recovery.</p></p>
