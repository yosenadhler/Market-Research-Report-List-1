<p><p>The automotive steering column market is primarily divided into two types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS) and Electric Power Steering (EPS). HPS utilizes hydraulic fluid to assist steering, providing a traditional feel but can be less efficient. EPS, on the other hand, employs an electric motor to enhance steering response and reduce energy consumption, making it increasingly popular due to its lightweight design and adaptability. Both types cater to different vehicle needs, impacting performance, fuel efficiency, and driver experience.</p></p>
