<p><p>The infrared camera market is primarily divided into two categories: cooled and uncooled infrared detectors. Cooled infrared detectors use cooling systems to reduce thermal noise, enabling high sensitivity and resolution, making them ideal for applications like military surveillance and scientific research. Uncooled infrared detectors, on the other hand, operate at ambient temperatures, providing a more compact, cost-effective solution suitable for commercial applications, such as automotive night vision and building inspections, where high sensitivity is less critical.</p></p>
