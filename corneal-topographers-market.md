<p><strong>The Corneal Topographers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pacido-based Systems</li><li>Evaluation-based Systems</li><li>Interferometric Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corneal topographers are vital tools in ophthalmology for assessing the corneal surface. Pacido-based systems use placido disks to capture images for mapping curvature, while evaluation-based systems utilize advanced algorithms to analyze topographical data, providing detailed insights into corneal shape and elevation. Interferometric systems, on the other hand, employ light interference to measure corneal thickness and surface irregularities with high precision. Together, these technologies enhance the diagnosis and management of various corneal disorders and refractive surgery planning.</p></p>
