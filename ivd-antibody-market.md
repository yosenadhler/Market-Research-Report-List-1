<p><p>The In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD) antibody market comprises three main types: monoclonal antibodies (mAbs), polyclonal antibodies (pAbs), and recombinant antibodies (rAbs). mAbs are identical copies of a single type of immune cell, providing high specificity for targets. pAbs, produced from various immune cells, recognize multiple epitopes, offering broader reactivity. rAbs, engineered through recombinant DNA technology, combine the advantages of mAbs and pAbs, allowing for tailored characteristics. Each type serves distinct purposes in diagnostics and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
