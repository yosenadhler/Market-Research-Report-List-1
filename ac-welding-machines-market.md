<p><ul><li>Moving Iron Core</li><li>Homo Style</li><li>Moving Coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC welding machines market includes three primary types: Moving Iron Core, Homo Style, and Moving Coil. Moving Iron Core machines utilize an iron core that shifts within a magnetic field, offering simplicity and reliability. Homo Style machines feature a homopolar design, providing consistent welding performance with reduced noise levels. Moving Coil machines use a coil that moves within a magnetic field, delivering precise control and improved efficiency. Each type serves different welding needs, influencing their applications in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
