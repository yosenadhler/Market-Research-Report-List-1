<p><p>Epoxy resin repair mortars are crucial for restoring and protecting surfaces in various applications. The market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and solvent-free variants. Solvent-based epoxies contain volatile organic compounds, providing excellent adhesion and flexibility but may have environmental concerns. In contrast, solvent-free epoxy mortars are more eco-friendly, offering lower toxicity and minimal odor, making them suitable for indoor applications. Both types are favored for their durability, resistance to chemicals, and ability to withstand harsh conditions.</p></p>
