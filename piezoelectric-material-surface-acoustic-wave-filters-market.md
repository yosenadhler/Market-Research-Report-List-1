<p><p>Piezoelectric material surface acoustic wave (SAW) filters are essential components in communication systems for signal processing. IF SAW filters are used for intermediate frequency applications, particularly in television and radio receivers, providing excellent selectivity and low insertion loss. RF SAW filters, on the other hand, operate at radio frequencies and are crucial in mobile and wireless communication systems, supporting the filtration of specific frequencies to improve signal clarity and reduce interference. Both types enhance performance in various electronic devices.</p></p>
