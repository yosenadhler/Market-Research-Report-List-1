<p><strong>The Sub-boiling Distillation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Quartz</li><li>Double Quartz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sub-boiling distillation systems are specialized setups for the purification of sensitive compounds at lower temperatures to prevent degradation. The market features two primary types: single quartz and double quartz systems. Single quartz systems use a single layer of quartz glass for heating, offering efficient distillation but limited thermal stability. In contrast, double quartz systems incorporate two layers of quartz, enhancing thermal insulation and control while enabling more precise temperature regulation and better separation efficiency for volatile components.</p></p>
