<p><p>Epitaxy equipment is crucial for the production of crystalline layers on substrates, with key market types including Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition (MOCVD) and High-Temperature Chemical Vapor Deposition (HT CVD). MOCVD is primarily used for fabricating compound semiconductors and optoelectronic devices, offering superior control over composition and thickness. HT CVD, on the other hand, operates at elevated temperatures, enabling the deposition of high-quality layers for advanced electronic and photonic applications, enhancing material properties and device performance.</p></p>
