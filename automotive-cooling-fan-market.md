<p><p>The automotive cooling fan market is segmented into three main types: radiator fans, condenser fans, and heat/ventilation fans. Radiator fans regulate engine temperature by cooling the coolant as it passes through the radiator. Condenser fans are essential for air conditioning systems, dissipating heat from the refrigerant to maintain efficient cooling. Heat/ventilation fans improve cabin airflow and comfort by circulating air within the vehicle. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance and passenger experience.</p></p>
