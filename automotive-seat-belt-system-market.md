<p><p>The automotive seat belt system market consists of two primary types: active and passive seat belt systems. Active seat belt systems include features like pretensioners and load limiters that automatically adjust to provide better protection in a crash, actively enhancing safety. In contrast, passive seat belt systems are the traditional belts that secure occupants during an accident but do not offer additional mechanisms for adjustment or tension. Both systems play crucial roles in improving occupant safety in vehicles.</p></p>
